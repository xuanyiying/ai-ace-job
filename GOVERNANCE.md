# Governance Model

The purpose of this document is to establish a formal governance model for the AI Resume Optimizer project. It clarifies how decisions are made and how community members can participate.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Users
Users are community members who have a need for the project. They provide important feedback in the form of bug reports and feature suggestions.

### Contributors
Contributors are community members who contribute in concrete ways to the project. This can take the form of code, documentation, design, or other contributions.

### Maintainers
Maintainers are contributors who have shown dedication to the project and have been granted write access to the repository. They are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues
- guiding the direction of the project
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct

### Project Lead
The Project Lead has the final say in decision-making if consensus cannot be reached.

## Decision Making Process

Decisions are made based on consensus among Maintainers. If consensus cannot be reached, the Project Lead will make the final decision.

Major technical decisions should be proposed via a Request for Comments (RFC) issue or a detailed Pull Request description.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have made significant contributions and demonstrated a commitment to the project may be invited to become Maintainers.

## Code of Conduct

All participants in the project are expected to adhere to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
